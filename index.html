About Our Firm

AA Mudanya & Co. Advocates is a premier corporate and commercial legal practice based in Nairobi, Kenya. Founded on principles of excellence, integrity, and client-focused service, we provide bespoke legal solutions to businesses and corporations across East Africa.

Our team combines deep market knowledge with innovative legal strategies to deliver results that exceed client expectations. We understand the complexities of the Kenyan business environment and provide practical, commercially sound advice.
